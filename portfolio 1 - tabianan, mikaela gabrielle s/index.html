Anawangin Cove and Lake Resort

San Antonio, Zambales

Want to go to a white beach but can’t go to Boracay? Anawangin Cove and Lake resort is a white beach and campsite resort located in Zambales. Enjoy the clear water beach and the hidden lake with fresh water

Book Now

Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park

Palawan

Located in Puerto Princesa, Palawan – is commonly known as Underground River and has been included in the UNESCO World Heritage Sites since 1999. Tour inside the cave to find beautiful stalactites and stalagmites structures, bats, fishes, and more.

Book Now

Mt. Kulis

Tanay, Rizal

Mt. Kulis is a beginner-friendly peak that provides picturesque vistas of the Sierra Madre mountain range as well as beautiful scenery from popular destinations. It became renowned among hikers and locals since it served as a shooting location for the ABS-CBN TV series "Ang Probinsyano" before it was officially accessible to the public.

Book Now

Vigan City

Ilocos Sur

Want to travel back in time? Take a trip to the Philippines oldest historical city – Vigan City. See houses and other structures from the time of the Spaniards still standing till this day. Try local cuisines, buy souvenirs, or find your favorite coffee shops and fast food branches in old structures.

Book Now

Kaparkan Falls

Abra

There are a number of beautiful waterfalls in the Philippines, however, non can compare to Kaparkan Falls. Natural limestones can be found surrounding the waterfalls. The journey to this beauty is hard, but all worth it. Ride the infamous “monster truck” that will take you 30 minutes to get there or trek for hours.
